Steps to Find Space Velocity (v):

Formula:-

u = (v+w) /(1 + (v * w)/c²)


where:
  • u is the speed of the projectile as seen outside of the spaceship.
  • v is the speed of the spaceship.
  • w is the speed of the projectile as seen from the spaceship.
  • c is the speed of light (299792458 m/s).
Input values are:-

Relative Projectile velocity (u) = {{rpv}} {{rpv_op}}

Projectile Velocity (w) = {{pv}} {{pv_op}}

Put values into formula:-

v = c² (u - w) / (c² - u*w)

v = 299792458² * ({{rpv}} - {{pv}}) / (299792458² - {{rpv}}*{{pv}})
