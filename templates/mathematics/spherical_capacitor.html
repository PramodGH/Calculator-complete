Formula:-

C = 4 * π * ε0 * εk / (1/a - 1/b)

  • C is the capacitance measured in farads (symbol: F)
  • ε0 is the vacuum permittivity - a constant value of 8.85 × 10^(−12) F/m (farads per meter)
  • εk is the relative permittivity. It is dimensionless
  • a is the radius of the inner sphere
  • b is the radius of the outer sphere
