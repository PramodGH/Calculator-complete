What is power with displacement ?

Power with respect to displacement states the amount of work done with a force to cause the displacement of the object in a particular time. Power is defined as the rate of the work done. It is denoted by symbol ‘P’. The unit of power is Watt (W).

Formula:-

Power(P) = (Force*Displacement)/Time


where:
  • F - Force applied
  • D - Displacement of object
  • T - Time taken
