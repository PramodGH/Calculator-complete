Procedure to Calculate Even Permutations

Go through the simple and easy guidelines to determine the Even Permutations. These instructions will make it easy for you to evaluate the Even Permutations. They are outlined as such

  • Firstly, find the factorial of the given number.
  • Remember the number should be greater than 2 i.e. n >2
  • Later divide the factorial obtained by 2 i.e. n!/ 2 to get the Even Permutations.
  • The resultant obtained is Even Permutations.

Example: Calculate Even Permutations for n =6?

Solution:

Given n = 6

Formula to find the Even Permutations = n!/ 2 for n > 2

Substituting the value of n in the formula we get as follows

Even Permutations = 6!/2

= (6*5*4*3*2*1)/2

= 720/2

= 360

Therefore, the number of ways that can be arranged for n = 6 is 360.
