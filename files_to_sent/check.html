Steps to Find Quarter Mile:

Formula:-

ET = 6.290 * (weight / power)¹ᐟ³

Final speed = 224 * (power / weight)¹ᐟ³


where:
  • ET is the elapsed time over a quarter-mile distance
  • final speed is the speed of the vehicle at the quarter-mile mark
  • 6.290 is an empirically derived constant
  • 224 is an empirically derived constant
  • weight is the total weight of the vehicle (including the driver) in pounds
  • power is the peak engine power at the clutch (net power) in horsepower
